What is Voyage Control?



Any business that sends or receives freight vehicles will benefit from our Voyage Control SaaS product.


Our product enables you complete control over your logistics, where you can control who arrives at your business,
what they deliver and when they deliver it.
Voyage Control provides you with the tools you need to manage your inbound deliveries, optimise your logistics and
track vehicles in real time.

Scheduling and opImising vehicle flow in busy hubs


Powerful scheduling tools to reduce waiting time for exhibitors; saving you money. Comprehensive reports to enable optimisation of operations based on planned activity.

Increased Security:


Security and management have constant access to a strong backend display of data, timeframes and real time tracking of their suppliers. With this information, security and management can monitor movement consistently with the reassurance of knowing what to expect eliminating the unknowing of unwanted arrivals or security threats.

Traceability, Data Gathering and Reports:


Waiting for deliveries can be time-consuming and expensive, with our real-time tracking you can monitor the whereabouts of your deliveries and manage your operations accordingly. The reports available allow you to measure past operations and manage future operations based on the data we collect.

Improve Customer Experience:


The software we provide enables a self-service experience for users, reducing their waiting time and enforcing a much smoother process.
With constant access to the Voyage Control helpdesk users are able to ask for help with queries and ultimately are able to plan their resources better.

Cutting fuel and CO2 emissions:


By reducing the idol waiting time of vehicles/freight we are reducing your waste of resources including fuel and manpower.
Coming soon is also the ability to optimise loads, reducing the number of vehicles travelling around with little to no cargo and having a huge positive impact on the environment and in reducing CO2 emissions.
